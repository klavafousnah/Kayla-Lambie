Content and Curriculum

A successful learning experience starts with good strategy and content. During this stage, we will identify key stakeholders and the objectives of your eLearning.

 

eLearning Design and Tools

Once the content is decided, we will need to decide how and what tools to use to display the content. This is where a good chunk of development time is spent and communication is key for exceptional results.

 

Development

This is where you get to see your ideas turn to life. This could be personalized modules, interactive documents, interactive micromodules, or whatever is needed to fit your needs.

Curious about who I am?

I am an educator and eLearning Developer with the ability to work with a variety of different tools. I started off with a B.Sc which led me into the teaching profession and obtaining my B.Ed. After this, I taught in both the UK and Canada as a Science Teacher. I received my M.A in curriculum development with a focus on STEM. I moved into curriculum development and eLearning and began to develop a wide array of digital skills.

  • Background in a variety of Education ideas including; eLearning, Coaching, and Science
  • Ability to create progressive and traditional education while meeting accessibility standards
  • Creative, passionate, and always looking for more things to learn.

I started out my passion for education by coaching and teaching Sunday school. From age 10, I was preparing myself to become the best educator and eLearning Developer possible.

Project information

  • Category: K-12
  • Client: Western University / High School
  • Project Date: 2019

Full teachable STEM PBL approach to teaching the SBI4U course.

There is little content that has been developed around STEM PBL. Within the project, I was able to create a robust program that included lessons, activities, final projects, rubrics and more. This allowed the teacher to focus on the students, not on the curriculum to be instructed. This was a new approach for the teacher and their students were more successful in this environment with this content compared to the teachers normal content.

Content was develeped by exploring the leaders in STEM PBL in education and assessing my content using those standards. I was also able to instruct the teacher how to successfully use these new resources and pedogogies.
